Foodwagon

Simple meal bandwagons

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Foodwagon plans your outings for you.

Ever have problems in the office trying to decide on where to go for lunch? Want to try a new place but you aren't sure who's interested? Simply create a FoodWagon in the channel and see who bandwagons along! You can even request a minimum number of people to come along for an event to happen (Such as a 5-person buffet special).

No More Clutter

No more need to sort through tons of spam in a #food channel. See all the upcoming plans and easy pick which you want to join. Nobody knows where to go? Grab a random restaurant from the suggestions!

Commands

Name | Syntax | Task ------------ | ------------- | ----------- Wagon | `/food TIME MIN_PEOPLE PLACE` | Makes a FoodWagon for people to join! Food | `/food TIME MIN_PEOPLE` | Grabs a random FoodWagon from the list of suggestions List | `/list` | View the list of restaurant suggestions Add | `/add PLACE` | Add a restaurant to the list of suggestions Join | `/join ID` | Enter the FoodWagon ID to confirm attendance Wagon List | `/wagons` | See the list of FoodWagons currently planned Help | `/help` | Access the documentation
